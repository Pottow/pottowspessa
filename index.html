Thesis Project

My thesis was a driving simulation experiment. It tested time-to-collision theories and steering laws, when approaching gates of variable width. The time it took each participant to complete the course was modelled using Accot and Zhai's Steering Law. The changing angle between the left gatepost, the driver, and the right gatepost at each instant was used as input for David Lee's TTC theory.
